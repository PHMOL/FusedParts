A Brief History


I am Philipp, an artist from Munich who is excited about urban street art and creating mixed media art. It has been about fifteen years since my first project back in school and I enjoyed working creatively ever since.

After some detours such as my Economic Studies and professional career in the financial sector, I decided to pursue my hobby in a more serious manner. Even though I don’t have an artistic background, I am engaged in all kinds of different techniques of modern art, so that I am able to transform my ideas into works of art. My first mission was to fuse various materials in my work, which is why I started with unicolor pictures and vinyl. I was really excited about the effect of vinyl on a canvas, so I kept experimenting with materials such as silk, wood, mirror, moss, resin or steel wool. At this point I try to use new materials in every piece of art and accentuate them with popping colors. My motives/themes are divers, although I currently focus on famous artists from the film and music industry as well as watercolor paintings of famous photography.

In the long run, I will also shift towards abstract art, so I can creatively display my ideas, values and worldview through my artwork.
